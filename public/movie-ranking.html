h1>The Best Movies According to Veronica

My top 3 movies of all time.

Gone With the Wind

Gone with the Wind is a historical epic based on the novel of the same name by Margaret Mitchell. It covers the Civil War and Reconstruction era from the point of view of a Southern debutante in Georgia.

The Nightmare Before Christmas

The Nightmare Before Christmas is a 1993 stop-motion animated film about Jack Skellington, the Pumpkin King of Halloween Town, who discovers Christmas Town and plots to take over Christmas

Pride and Prejudice

Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen is a romantic comedy that explores themes of social class, reputation, and love versus marriage.
